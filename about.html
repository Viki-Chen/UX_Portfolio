Hi! I am Viki Chen, a Product Designer located in Santa Barbara, California. I am passionate about finding creative solutions to complex problems.

During my time in legal tech, I excelled in creating intuitive human-centric designs and optimizing complex workflows.
Why UX design? This field allows me to express my creativity, to problem solve, and to collectively build innovative products. With design, I can experience the thrill of solving a puzzle while giving life to my own creative vision at the same time.
When I am not designing you can find me traveling the world, or at home baking and crafting delicious new recipes!
